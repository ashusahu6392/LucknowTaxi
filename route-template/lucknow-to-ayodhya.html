Book Lucknow to Ayodhya Taxi for Ram Mandir Darshan

Planning a trip to the holy city of Ayodhya? Lucknow Taxi & Rental makes your pilgrimage simple and comfortable with reliable cabs from Lucknow to Ayodhya. The roughly 135 km journey on NH-27 takes about 3 hours, so you can leave Lucknow early and reach in time for morning darshan at the Ram Mandir.

Choose a budget sedan, a spacious SUV, the premium Innova Crysta, or a Tempo Traveller for a family group or community trip. Our same-day round-trip option is extremely popular with devotees — your car and driver wait while you complete darshan and bring you back to Lucknow the same day. Toll, parking and driver allowance are quoted upfront so your pilgrimage stays stress-free.

Lucknow to Ayodhya Distance & Time

The distance is approximately 135 km and the drive usually takes around 3 hours via the well-maintained NH-27. Early-morning starts are recommended to make the most of your day in Ayodhya.
