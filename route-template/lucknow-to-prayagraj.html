Book Lucknow to Prayagraj Taxi for Sangam Darshan

Prayagraj (Allahabad), home to the holy Triveni Sangam, is one of the most visited pilgrimage destinations from Lucknow. Lucknow Taxi & Rental offers dependable cabs for this ~200 km journey, which takes about 4 hours on the NH-30 / Purvanchal corridor. Whether you are travelling for Sangam snan, Kumbh, university work or a family visit, our door-to-door service makes the trip easy.

Pick a sedan for a small group, an SUV or Innova Crysta for extra comfort on the longer drive, or a Tempo Traveller for larger groups. During Kumbh and Magh Mela we run additional vehicles — we recommend booking early as demand is high. All fares, tolls and driver allowances are confirmed upfront on WhatsApp.

Lucknow to Prayagraj Distance & Time

The distance is approximately 200 km and the journey takes around 4 hours. Roads are good for most of the stretch; an early start helps you reach the ghats in time for morning rituals.
