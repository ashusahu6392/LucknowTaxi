Book Lucknow to Kanpur Taxi at Best Fare

Travelling from Lucknow to Kanpur? Lucknow Taxi & Rental offers the most reliable and affordable cab service on this popular route. The two cities are connected by the well-maintained NH-27 highway, making the roughly 90 km journey quick and comfortable — usually around 2 hours depending on traffic. Whether you are heading to Kanpur for business, education, a family visit or onward travel, our door-to-door taxi service gets you there on time.

We offer a range of vehicles to match your needs and budget — economical sedans like the Dzire and Aura for solo travellers and small families, spacious SUVs such as the Ertiga and XL6 for bigger groups, and the premium Innova Crysta for maximum comfort on the highway. For group travel, our Tempo Traveller is ideal. You can choose a one-way drop and pay only for a single side, or a round trip with the same car and driver for your return.

Lucknow to Kanpur Distance & Travel Time

The Lucknow to Kanpur road distance is approximately 90 kilometres via NH-27 (the Lucknow–Kanpur Road / upcoming expressway corridor). In normal traffic, the drive takes about 2 hours. Early-morning and late-evening departures are usually the fastest. Our drivers plan the route to avoid peak congestion around Lucknow and the Kanpur city entry.

One Way & Round Trip Options

For one-way travel, you pay only for the single journey — perfect if you don't need a return. For round trips, the same vehicle waits and brings you back, which is cost-effective for day trips and meetings. Outstation charges, driver allowance, toll and parking are shared upfront so there are never any surprises.
